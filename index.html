"Friends of MS Charities" are no friends of MS

Friends of MS Charities claims they give 80% of donations to fighting MS

That's not true, according to Charity Navigator and this KSL article on their organization.

Of the nearly $2.4 million they've raised in the last two years, here's what "Friends of MS Charities" actually spends their donations on:

  • 16% - Program Expenses (actual donations to MS causes)
  • 16.7% - Administrative Expenses (Money paid to employeees)
  • 67.2% - Fundraising Expenses

Of the last two years, they've given the reputable National MS Society $8,500. That's 0.3% of their donations. That falls short of their 80% claim by, well, 79.7%.
